Remember that older elephants can teach younger elephants (this is you using your memory!).

Replay Sequence
If a group of elephants go near a hive,
the guard bees fly out of the hive. The buzzing noise scares the older elephants. They warn the other elephants by making a
rumbling noise and then run away. The young elephants hear the bees buzzing
and the elephants rumbling. They see the older elephants run away and also
run away.
